About

Freanel daycare is a specialized childcare center dedicated to fostering holistic development in children by integrating a strong connection to nature. Through engaging outdoor activities and nature-based experiences, children at Freanel daycare develop a deeper appreciation for the environment while enhancing their communication skills. By intertwining nature exploration and effective communication, Freanel daycare cultivates an enriching environment that nurtures children's cognitive, emotional, and social growth.
